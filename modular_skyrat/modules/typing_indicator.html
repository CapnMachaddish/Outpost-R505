Title: Typing Indicator

MODULE ID: TYPING_INDICATOR

Description:

Adds an asynchronously tracked typing indicator, which shows a typing bubble with an animation. Added in a hacky way to make it very smooth on the user's end. It registers "T" and "M" keypresses for the indicator to popup, and makes it gone on mouse click, or say/emote send

TG Proc Changes:

  • APPEND: code/modules/keybindings/setup.dm > /datum/proc/key_down()
  • ADDITION: code/onclick/_click.dm > /mob/proc/ClickOn()
  • ADDITION: code/modules/mob/mob_say.dm > /mob/verb/say_verb(), /mob/verb/me_verb()
  • CHANGE: code\modules\mob\living\living_say.dm > /mob/living/send_speech
